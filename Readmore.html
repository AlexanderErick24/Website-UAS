About Me

My name is Alexander Erick, and I am currently a 2nd-semester student pursuing my studies at Institut Sains dan Teknologi Terpadu Surabaya (ISTTS). Born and raised in Surabaya on February 24, 2005, I have always been deeply rooted in the vibrant culture and dynamic atmosphere of this bustling city. My family, consisting of loving parents and a younger sister, serves as a constant source of joy and support in my life journey.

From a young age, I have harbored a profound fascination for the intricate world of technology and business, with a particular penchant for information technology. The rapid advancements in software and digital technologies have captivated my curiosity since childhood, igniting within me a desire to explore and understand the ever-evolving landscape of technological innovation.

It is this innate passion and curiosity that propelled me to pursue a major in Business Information Systems at ISTTS. Through my academic endeavors, I am eager to acquire a comprehensive understanding of the multifaceted aspects of information technology, ranging from data analysis to software development, with the ultimate goal of honing my skills and expertise in this dynamic field.

However, my aspirations extend beyond personal development. I firmly believe in the transformative power of technology to effect positive change in society. With a deep-seated commitment to leveraging technology for the greater good, I am driven to explore innovative solutions that address pressing societal challenges and enhance the quality of life for individuals and communities alike.

In essence, my journey in technology and business is not merely a pursuit of knowledge but a steadfast commitment to making a meaningful impact. I am dedicated to harnessing the boundless potential of technology to drive positive change and contribute to the collective advancement of society. Through unwavering determination and a steadfast belief in the power of innovation, I aspire to leave a lasting legacy of progress and transformation in the world around me.
