Top Artist in 2023

Pedro Pascal

It comes as no surprise that Pedro Pascal is the top star of the year. The Chilean-born actor played the lead in two of 2023’s most popular TV series to critical acclaim. Audiences couldn’t get enough of Pascal as the gruff survivor Joel in “The Last of Us,” returning weekly to watch as his character grew from reluctant protector to apocalyptic hero. While over on Disney+, the actor continued delighting fans as the enigmatic Mandalorian in Season 3 of the Star Wars spin-off. His work this year has earned him three Emmy Award nominations and led him to spend 13 weeks in IMDb’s STARmeter Top 10.

Mary Elizabeth Winstead

As part of the trifecta of women at the center of Star Wars series “Ahsoka,” Mary Elizabeth Winstead shot to the top of IMDb’s STARmeter, staying in the top five through the entire season. Behind her green Twi’lek makeup, Winstead portrayed Hera Syndulla, a character familiar to fans of the animated “Star Wars: Rebels.” To close out the year, the actress lent her voice to the animated ”Scott Pilgrim Takes Off,” playing the same character she portrayed in the live action Scott Pilgrim vs. the World 13 years ago.

Ana de Armas

Ana de Armas is no stranger to IMDb's Top Stars list, having held court in the top five since 2020. Interest for the actress stayed high through awards season for her portrayal of Marilyn Monroe in Blonde. Then, de Armas reunited with her Knives Out co-star Chris Evans in the Apple TV+ action comedy Ghosted, which (despite mixed reviews), catapulted her to the top of STARmeter for three weeks. With the John Wick spinoff Ballerina set to premiere next year, don't be surprised if she continues her Top Stars reign in 2024.
